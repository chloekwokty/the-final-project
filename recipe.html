Green Thai Curry Tofu

  • Cooking time: 25 minutes
  • Serving: 4 people

Ingredients:

  • Jasmine rice 240g
  • Smoked tofu 225g pack, cubed
  • Vegetable oil 2 tspy
  • Thai green curry paste 50g
  • Low-fat coconut milk 400g tin
  • Sugar snap peas 150g
  • Baby sweetcorn 175g, halved lengthways
  • Green beans 150g, halved
  • Lime 1, juiced
  • Coriander a small bunch, leaves picked

Vegan Aubergine Burgers

  • Cooking time: 40-60 minutes
  • Serving: 4 people

Ingredients:

  • 4 wholewheat or multiseed bread rolls (each weighing about 75g/2¾oz), halved
  • 1 large aubergine, wide middle section cut into 4 slices 2cm/¾in thick
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp ground coriander
  • 1 small red pepper, deseeded and finely sliced
  • 1 small yellow pepper, deseeded and finely sliced
  • 1 red onion, finely sliced
  • 1½ tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 25g/1oz fresh rocket leaves or baby leaf salad
  • 1 large beef tomato, cut into four thick slices
